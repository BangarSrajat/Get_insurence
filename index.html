What is Car Insurance?

Car insurance, also referred to as motor or auto insurance, is an insurance policy that provides you financial protection if your vehicle suffers damages due to accidents, natural disasters, or thefts.

With a Comprehensive Car Insurance Policy, you will be financially secured in case of any losses to your vehicle due to an unfortunate event. Apart from that, a private car insurance policy also covers third-party liabilities and ensures that you comply with the law of the land.
